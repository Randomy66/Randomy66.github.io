Invite Randomy to your server!

1. Go to "https://discord.com/oauth2/authorize?client_id=1127268850732376184" by clicking "Go Now" or scanning the "QR code".

2. Login to your discord account {If isn't already log in}

3. Click "Add to Server"

4. Choose the Server that you want to add Randomy, click continue

5. Check the box that you want to grant Randomy following permissions. "Note that some feature won't work if you don't grant all permissions." Click Authorize.

And All Done!
